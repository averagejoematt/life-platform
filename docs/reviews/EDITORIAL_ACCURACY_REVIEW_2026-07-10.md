# Editorial Accuracy Review — 2026-07-10 (the stall audit)

**Trigger:** ~15 days of manual-logging silence (food since 06-24, lifting since 06-25, habits since 06-27, journal since 05-25, weigh-ins since 06-26) while Whoop/EightSleep kept flowing. Question: does the platform, as rendered today, tell a fresh reader the truth about that?

**Method:** `/accuracy-review full` — site_review capture (34 pages, 51 endpoints) → Axis A deterministic audit (cross-page consistency, API↔DDB ground truth, sentinel scan) → 12-cluster prose-grounding workflow (47 agents), every critical/high finding adversarially verified against the capture + repo + live DDB. Only verified findings are listed. 1 finding refuted in verification.

## Top-line verdict

**Split verdict.** The AI narrative layer largely names the stall — the mind/nutrition/integrator coaches, the chronicle ("the person inside the body left no testimony"), panelcast EP4, `/api/presence` (public: `dark`, gap 15d), adaptive mode ("Rough Patch"), and the character page's quiet-stretch banner are honest, some of them excellent. The failures are concentrated in (a) the **deterministic/front-end layer**, which still narrates a live program over dead sources (training "ENGINE 145%", nutrition "uploads after the day ends", "LBS TODAY" off a 14-day-old weigh-in, "Rest day" on blackout day 15, family panel "eased off a little"), (b) **aggregate windows** that dilute the last 15 days into 4-week averages, (c) the **gamification layer**, which leveled the character UP 8→13 with two tier-up celebrations during total neglect (up-gate scale bug), and (d) two **privacy leaks** unrelated to the stall but caught by the sweep. Axis A numeric checks: weight/HRV/RHR/level all API↔DDB consistent; the lies are framing, windows, and absence-handling — not raw numbers.

**Counts:** 34 confirmed serious findings (5 critical, 29 high) + 57 medium/low.
