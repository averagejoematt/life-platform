## Review questions to apply to every page

These are the spirit of every page review (not every question needs answering for every page):
- Does the page purpose make sense? Do you understand what it's doing?
- Is the content unique or repetitive with other pages?
- Is there content or information you expected but is missing?
- This is a human story, a science story, an AI story — does the page blend the right amount for its purpose? Should there be more narrative, more data, more story through data?
- How appealing is the page visually? Can you extrapolate information easily? Are there opportunities for more striking graphics or visuals?
- From a reader perspective — should elements be interactive or allow feedback?
- Knowing the innovative world of AI, could we be doing more with this page?
- Imagine 6 months of data — would this page be adequate? If not, what should change?
- Anything else that would make this a brilliant, gripping website that has readers wanting to keep coming back, with commercialization opportunity down the road?
