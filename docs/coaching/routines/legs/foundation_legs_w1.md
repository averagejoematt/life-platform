# Foundation — Legs — W1  *(re-entry, on-ramp)*

- **Phase / type / tier:** Foundation / Lower / Tier 1
- **Hevy routine_id:** a98f6295-0e6e-42b5-86da-93df6eb854a8
- **Target date:** 2026-06-18
- **Status:** committed
- **Context:** first lower-body training in 9+ months. Submaximal re-entry, 3 RIR, feeler
  loads (no leg history to anchor). Squat + RDL chosen as real stimulus; RDL over trap bar to
  avoid re-loading traps/grip from pull day.

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Load | Rest | Note (intent) |
|---|----------|-------------|------|------|----------------|
| 1 | Squat (Barbell) | 3 × 8 | 135 lb *(feeler)* | 180s | PRIMARY. Heels wedged or to a box. Parallel-ish, braced flat back, 3 RIR. Climb only if it flies. |
| 2 | Romanian Deadlift (Barbell) | 3 × 10 | 135 lb *(feeler)* | 150s | Hinge, soft knees, short ROM. Strap up if grip fades from pull day. 3 RIR. |
| 3 | Leg Press (Machine) | 2 × 12 | 180 lb | 120s | Accessory quad volume, lower skill — safe to push closer to failure. |
| 4 | Seated Leg Curl | 2 × 12 | 70 lb | 90s | Hamstring isolation, full ROM. ⚠ Fix this exercise's muscle group to **Hamstrings** in Hevy. |
| 5 | Standing Calf Raise | 3 × 15 | 90 lb | 60s | Pause top + bottom. |
| 6 | Cycling | 30 min | — | — | Easy Zone 2 spin — genuinely easy, legs are cooked. Flush, not a workout. |

**Progression levers next time:** loads become performance-gated once a baseline exists
(climb off the feeler); after the ~2-wk on-ramp, open squat/RDL toward working weight.
