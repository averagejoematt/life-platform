## CHAPTER 5: WHY PUBLIC

### Q14: Why make it public?
- **The cheerleader insight**: "Since my mum passed, I lost my cheerleader. If I get promoted, if I do anything, nobody really actually cares. My brother, my dad, it might be a small text, or a textbook follow-up question — but I don't feel as seen about what my life is to anyone anymore. My mum gave that. So maybe this site is trying to find my cheerleaders."
- **The inspiration desire**: "Some sort of desire to actually inspire others who struggle. I genuinely don't know many, though through Reddit I know it exists, and I think I'd love to actually be a motivating factor in future — I am passionate about a lot of this. Even working out — I love it — that's what's so odd when I stop."
- **The commercial dream**: "I think what I would dream of is making this a commercial venture that is actually an awesome value add and product. In early chats, it doesn't feel too viable right now to package this up and give to others. But I'd love if everyone used AJM or whatever the rebrand was to also get the same motivation, challenges, experiences, data and everything that I do. And I want them to want to do it, because they have all the quantitative data paired with my journey — to see how well it worked as I myself prove I got happier while losing weight, through this platform's support."

### Q15: Who is this site for?
- "Right now probably people trying to lose weight"
- "In the future, I'd love it to be more for all fitness/exercise enthusiasts/data geeks for this type of stuff"

### Q16: What does 185 represent beyond a number?
- "It's just when I felt so much more myself"
- "Sports, exercise, socializing, nothing was out of reach"
- "I try to remind myself I do not want to go back to that person, that is not realistic. But I felt so much more normal."
- "For the couple of weeks I had that last year in May — I was not perfect, I still had my anxiety and social awkwardness — but I could tell I was much better!"

I implemented a sick mode in this platform, and then I started logging sick days again and again. And then when I felt better, I wanted a couple of days to just catch back to fully normal.
