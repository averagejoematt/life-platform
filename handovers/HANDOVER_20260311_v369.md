### TB7-10: Reserved Concurrency Investigation — Closed N/A ✅

**Finding:** `aws lambda get-account-settings` returned `ConcurrentExecutions: 10`. This is the account-level floor. AWS requires a minimum of 10 unreserved executions at all times. Setting `reserved_concurrent_executions=1` on any Lambda violates this constraint — CloudFormation UPDATE_FAILED on 3 Lambdas before rollback.

**Decision:** Reserved concurrency removed from ingestion_stack.py and lambda_helpers.py. Not applicable at this account's concurrency ceiling. EventBridge fires exactly one invocation per schedule; concurrent OAuth token races are not a real risk. Documented in PROJECT_PLAN TB7-10.

**CDK files net-zero:** ingestion_stack.py and lambda_helpers.py were modified then reverted — no net change from v3.7.0 baseline.
