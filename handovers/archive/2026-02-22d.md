### v2.2.0 — Three core MacroFactor tools

**`get_nutrition_summary`**
- Daily macro breakdown + period averages for calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sodium, caffeine, omega-3, potassium, magnesium, vitamin D
- Computes protein % of calories per day
- Gap analysis vs reference targets
- Query: `get_date_range` pattern on `macrofactor` source

**`get_macro_targets`**
- Daily adherence scoring vs calorie and protein goals
- Auto-estimates TDEE from latest Withings weight (Mifflin-St Jeor × 1.55 activity factor)
- Calorie and protein targets overridable via args
- Returns hit/miss per day + overall adherence rates (calorie, protein, fiber)

**`get_food_log`**
- Single-day food diary with per-item macros and daily totals
- Reads `food_log` nested list from DynamoDB item
- Default: yesterday

### v2.3.0 — Three longevity-grade tools

**`get_micronutrient_report`**
- Scores ~25 nutrients against RDA and longevity-optimal targets
- Reference data: NIH DRI (2024), Attia protocol, Rhonda Patrick, Bryan Johnson Blueprint
- Categories: Macros, Fatty Acids, Minerals, Vitamins, B Vitamins, Other
- Status flags: DEFICIENT (<60% RDA), LOW (60–90%), ADEQUATE, ABOVE_UPPER_LIMIT
- Computes omega-6:omega-3 ratio vs 4:1 Attia/Simopoulos target
- Auto-generates longevity commentary: DHA <1g/day, magnesium <350mg/day, vitamin D PNW risk
- Reference constants: `_MICRONUTRIENT_TARGETS`, `_OMEGA_RATIO_TARGET` (4.0), `_LEUCINE_MPS_THRESHOLD` (2.5g)

**`get_meal_timing`**
- Eating window per day: first bite, last bite, window hours
- Caloric distribution: morning (<11am), midday (11–15), evening (15–20), late (>20)
- Circadian consistency: SD of first/last bite times across the period
- Late eating flag: last bite ≥ 20:00
- Sleep overlap: queries Eight Sleep for sleep onset, computes last-bite → sleep gap
- TRF status: OPTIMAL (≤10h), BORDERLINE (10–12h), WIDE (>12h)
- Flags: wide window, inconsistent first bite, >30% late-eating days, <2.5h pre-sleep gap

**`get_nutrition_biometrics_correlation`**
- Tests 90 nutrition-biometric pairs (10 × 9)
- Nutrition fields: calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, omega-3, sodium, caffeine, magnesium, alcohol
- Biometric outcomes: HRV, recovery score, resting HR, sleep performance, strain (Whoop); weight (Withings); sleep score, efficiency, sleep HRV (Eight Sleep)
- Optional `lag_days` (default 1): shifts biometrics forward to test next-day effects
- Pearson r: strong ≥0.5, moderate ≥0.35, weak ≥0.2; only ≥0.2 reported
- Returns: top_findings (top 15 by |r|), actionable_findings (strong/moderate only), all_results
- Requires ≥14 days of overlapping MacroFactor + biometric data
