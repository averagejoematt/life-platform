Why Genome-Informed?

Genetic variants affect how you absorb, convert, and utilise nutrients. A 110-SNP analysis identified three variants in Matthew's genome that directly influence supplementation decisions: the TRPM6 variant affecting magnesium transport, VDR Bsm1 reducing Vitamin D receptor binding efficiency (requiring higher-than-standard D3 dosing), and FADS2 rs1535 which impairs ALA-to-EPA/DHA conversion — making pre-formed Omega-3 (fish oil rather than flaxseed) the correct choice. Standard supplement advice ignores these differences. The genome analysis doesn't replace clinical lab work — both are required to dial in the protocol.
