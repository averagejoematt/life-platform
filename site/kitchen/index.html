The Kitchen

Personalized meal recommendations built from continuous glucose monitoring, macro targets, and your actual eating patterns. Not generic advice — your data, your meals, your body.

The Concept
📉
CGM-scored meals
Every meal you eat gets a glucose response grade from your Dexcom Stelo data. The Kitchen learns which foods are your body’s A+ foods — not population averages.
🎯
Macro-optimized
Recommendations prioritize protein density and leucine thresholds. Every suggestion hits your macro targets, not just calorie counts.
🔄
Built from your patterns
The Kitchen doesn’t suggest foods you’ll never eat. It works with meals you already love, optimizing timing, portions, and combinations.
How It Works
01
Ingest your food log — MacroFactor data is pulled nightly. Every meal, every macro, every timestamp.
02
Score against CGM — Each meal is matched to your Dexcom Stelo glucose response. Post-meal spike, AUC, and recovery time.
03
Rank by protein efficiency — Protein-per-calorie ratio identifies meals that maximize muscle protein synthesis per unit of energy.
04
Generate daily plans — Context-aware meal suggestions based on your training schedule, remaining macros, and time of day.
05
Adapt continuously — As your TDEE shifts and food responses change, The Kitchen evolves with you.
Most meal plans are built from population averages. The Kitchen is built from your bloodstream.
