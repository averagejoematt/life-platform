Under the hood

How It Works

  • 01 Persistent memory. Each coach maintains a continuous thread of analysis across weeks. They remember what they said, track whether it held up, and revise their positions when the data warrants it.
  • 02 Domain expertise. Eight specialists — sleep, nutrition, training, mind, body composition, glucose, labs, and cross-domain statistics — each constrained to their domain's data and analytical voice.
  • 03 Cross-domain awareness. Coaches see each other's analyses. When Dr. Park notices sleep quality dropping, Dr. Chen can factor that into training load recommendations. Disagreements are tracked.
  • 04 Prediction tracking. Coaches make explicit predictions with confidence levels. The system tracks outcomes — confirmed, refuted, or still pending. Accountability is structural, not optional.
  • 05 Integrator synthesis. Dr. Kai Nakamura reviews all eight coaches weekly, identifies the single highest-priority action, and issues a unified directive. One voice from many.
