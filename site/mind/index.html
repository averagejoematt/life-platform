Building this measurement
Journal data is growing. As entries accumulate, this section will show cognitive pattern distribution, mood-energy divergence detection, burnout early warning, and values alignment. The gap here is honest — this is the hardest thing to measure and the most important to get right.

The fifth pillar
everyone avoids

Attia added emotional health as his fifth pillar after decades of hiding behind data. Chronic psychological stress elevates cortisol, suppresses immune function, disrupts sleep, and accelerates cellular aging.

You can optimize sleep, glucose, and training perfectly. If you’re miserable, it erodes all of it. This page is intentionally incomplete. Showing the gaps honestly is part of the project.

Measurement Protocol
HOW WE FEELMood valence via Apple Health. Scale: -1 (very unpleasant) to +1 (very pleasant). Synced via webhook.
JOURNAL ANALYSISNotion entries enriched by Haiku: mood, energy, stress, cognitive patterns (CBT), themes, emotions, social quality (PERMA).
VICE STREAKSHabitify-tracked behavioral commitments. Compounding value: streak^1.5/10. Identity threshold: day 30. N=1.
