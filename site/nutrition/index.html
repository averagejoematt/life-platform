Losing weight without
losing muscle

The single biggest risk in aggressive weight loss is losing lean mass alongside fat. Protein adequacy is the primary defense — 1.6–2.2g per kg of lean body mass preserves muscle during a caloric deficit.

MacroFactor tracks every meal. The platform ingests this nightly and computes adherence rates, TDEE estimates, and cross-references meals with CGM glucose data to score each food by its metabolic impact.
