a measured life · proof, not promises

An honest documentary of an ordinary life, rebuilt with AI.

One regular guy, the wearables he already owns, and an AI board reading every number he produces — published either way, down weeks shown, not hidden. The whole site is one feedback loop. Scroll it once and you know how everything here works.

the loop closes · protocols shift the data

That's the whole site — and it's running now.

The ordinary life is Matthew's — sixteen prior climbs, sixteen collapses, and a drawer of devices that measured everything and changed nothing. What's different this time is the loop above: every number read, argued over, and published either way.

This attempt starts at the Day‑1 weigh‑in, aimed at 185 lbs held for 90 consecutive days — or the checkpoint fails, on the record. The model tracks the odds, collapses included. The scale is an instrument, not the finish line.

No million-dollar lab. Just the wearables already on his body, and a model that reads the numbers back to him every morning.
