The experiment

Why I'm doing this in public

I'm a senior director at a SaaS company in Seattle. In January 2026 I decided to actually change — not with a new app or a 30-day challenge, but by building the infrastructure to track everything and letting the data drive the decisions.

Every morning an AI reads 19 data sources and writes me a coaching brief. Every Wednesday a journalist named Elena Voss chronicles the week. Every week I publish the real numbers — the bad days included.
