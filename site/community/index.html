I built a whole page about my inner life because Dr. Vivek Murthy’s research on loneliness convinced me that social connection is a health metric, not a lifestyle bonus. The data backs this up — social isolation carries the same mortality risk as smoking 15 cigarettes a day.

This community exists because the experiment shouldn’t be a solo project. If you’re tracking your own health data, running your own experiments, or just curious about what N=1 means in practice — there’s a seat at the table.
