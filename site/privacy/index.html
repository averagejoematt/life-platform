Policy

// last updated march 2026

TL;DR: I collect your email address to send you the weekly signal. I don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or send you anything other than what you signed up for. You can unsubscribe anytime. That's it.

// who this is

This site — averagejoematt.com — is a personal project run by Matthew Walker, a solo developer in Seattle. It is not a company. There are no advertisers, no investors, no affiliate arrangements.

Questions or requests: matt@averagejoematt.com

// what I collect

When you subscribe, I collect:

  • Email address — required to send the weekly signal.
  • Why you're here / how you found this — optional free-text fields on the subscribe form. Helps me understand the audience. Never shared.
  • Subscribe source — which page or referral link you used.
  • Confirmation timestamp — when you confirmed your double opt-in.

I do not collect your name unless you volunteer it. I do not collect payment information — this newsletter is free. I do not use tracking pixels or third-party analytics in the emails.

// how I use it

Your email address is used for exactly one thing: sending you the weekly signal you signed up for. I will never sell or share your email with any third party, advertiser, or sponsor.

// where data lives

Subscriber data is stored in Amazon DynamoDB (AWS us-west-2, encrypted at rest). Email delivery is handled via Amazon SES. No subscriber data is stored in any third-party marketing platform or CRM.

// unsubscribing

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Click it and you're removed immediately — no re-engagement emails, no questions asked. You can also email matt@averagejoematt.com and I'll remove you within 24 hours.

After unsubscribing, your email is deleted from the subscriber list entirely.

// cookies and tracking

This site uses no tracking cookies and no third-party analytics. There is no Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or equivalent.

// your rights

You can request a copy of any data I hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion. Email matt@averagejoematt.com and I'll respond within 7 days.

// changes

Material changes will be notified to subscribers before taking effect. The last-updated date above reflects the current version.
