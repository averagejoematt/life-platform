The Game

This isn't a weight tracker. It's an RPG for real life. Seven pillars, each scored from live wearable and biometric data — no self-reporting. The score is honest, and it's public, because accountability to an audience is itself a performance variable.

Every night, 19 data sources feed a scoring engine that grades yesterday across Sleep, Movement, Nutrition, Metabolic, Mind, Social, and Consistency. The composite drives level progression on a logarithmic XP curve — early levels come fast, higher levels demand sustained performance across all pillars.

Methodology

How scores are computed

Each pillar's raw score (0–100) is computed nightly from real wearable, habit, and biometric data — not self-reported. Scores feed into an XP system that determines character level.

Pillar weights are configurable but currently equal (14.3% each). The composite Character Score is the weighted average. Level progression uses a logarithmic XP curve — early levels come fast, higher levels require sustained performance.

Full algorithm: see platform architecture

The Math

Each pillar's raw score (0-100) feeds into the XP engine nightly. The composite Character Score is the mean of all 7 pillar scores.

XP per day = composite_score / 10, accumulated into a lifetime total. Level is determined by a logarithmic curve: level = floor(sqrt(xp_total / 5)). This means early levels (1-5) come within the first few weeks, but higher levels (20+) require months of sustained, across-the-board performance.

Tier thresholds: Foundation (Lv 1-20), Momentum (Lv 21-40), Discipline (Lv 41-60), Mastery (Lv 61-80), Elite (Lv 81-100). Pillar weights are currently equal at 14.3% each.

Badges are evaluated nightly against streak counts, level milestones, weight thresholds, and data consistency targets. No manual overrides.
