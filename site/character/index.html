The Character Sheet

This isn't a weight tracker. It's an experiment: can you quantify whether someone is actually becoming a better version of themselves? Seven pillars. Each measured from real data. No self-reporting. The score is honest, and it's public — because accountability to an audience is itself a performance variable.

Every night, 19 data sources feed a scoring engine that grades yesterday across 7 pillars — Sleep, Movement, Nutrition, Metabolic, Mind, Social, Consistency. Each pillar has a raw score of 0–100. The composite becomes your Character Score, which drives level progression on a logarithmic XP curve.

The tier system — Foundation → Momentum → Chisel → Elite — requires not just a high score today, but sustained performance over multiple weeks. A single great day doesn't level you up. A month of great days does.

// what each tier means
Foundation Building consistent habits. Weeks 1–4. Most people live here permanently.
Momentum Habits are automated. Progress is measurable. The compound curve starts.
Chisel Optimizing from a stable base. Active experiments running.
Elite All 7 pillars performing simultaneously. The rarest state.

Methodology

How scores are computed

Each pillar’s raw score (0–100) is computed nightly from real wearable, habit, and biometric data — not self-reported. Scores feed into an XP system that determines character level.

Pillar weights are configurable but currently equal (14.3% each). The composite Character Score is the weighted average of all 7 pillars. Level progression uses a logarithmic XP curve — early levels come fast, higher levels require sustained performance.

// pillar data sources
Sleep Whoop sleep score, Eight Sleep data, sleep duration + efficiency
Movement Strava/Garmin activities, Zone 2 minutes, ACWR ratio, step count
Nutrition MacroFactor calories, protein target, deficit compliance
Metabolic Withings weight, Dexcom CGM glucose, blood pressure, labs
Mind Notion journal sentiment, state-of-mind log, Habitify mindfulness habits
Social Interaction log frequency + depth, journal social references
Consist. Habitify T0 streak, vice streak days, Todoist completion rate

// N=1 DISCLAIMER
This is a single-person experiment. Correlations observed in this data may not apply to anyone else. I track everything to make better decisions for myself, not to prove universal principles. Where I note that X correlates with Y, I mean in my data, during this period, with confounders I can’t fully control for.
