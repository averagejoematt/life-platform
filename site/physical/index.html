Methodology
Withings Scale
Daily weigh-ins via Withings Body+ smart scale. Weight synced to DynamoDB via API. Raw readings — no smoothing applied at ingestion.
Moving Average
7-day rolling average computed client-side from raw daily readings. Smooths water weight fluctuations to reveal the real trajectory.
Weekly Rate
Linear regression on the most recent 28 days of data. Projected goal date extrapolates from this rate assuming sustained adherence.
